Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel or change my car reservation if my plans change?

Some car reservations may be cancelled without a penalty. Log in to your My Account on Hotwire. If you have the option to cancel, a "Cancel reservation" button will appear next to your reservation. If you decide to cancel, you'll receive an email confirmation.

Can I add rooms to my booked reservation?

You sure can! To add a room or additional nights to an existing reservation, use our "Add to Your Stay" feature. Provided the hotel has availability, you'll be booked an additional reservation. This means you'll have two bookings at the same hotel with separate confirmation emails, confirmation codes and credit card charges. Your original reservation will remain unchanged.
